About Love Lucy Beauty

Love Lucy Beauty provides high quality treatments in the comfort of your own home OR in my gorgeous treatment room where you can relax and be pampered in a salon-style setting.

I offer a wide range of services from manicures and pedicures to facials, lash and brow treatments and am available for daytime, evening and weekend appointments.

I specialise in Swedish body massage and holistic treatments such as Indian head massage and hot stone therapy.

I am qualified with a VTCT NVQ Level 3 Diploma in Beauty Therapy and a VTCT Level 2 Diploma in Beauty Specialist Techniques obtained from Omni Academy of Beauty, a private college in Walton-on-Thames. I am also a fully insured member of the British Association of Beauty Therapy & Cosmetology (BABTAC) and a member of the Federation of Holistic Therapists.

I am also a professional make up artist and am available for weddings, proms, fancy dress and special occasions. You can see samples of my work on my Make Up by Lucy website or on my Love Lucy Beauty Facebook page.
